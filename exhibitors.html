Exhibitors

    We invite vendors to display their products and services at the Interplanetary Small Satellite Conference! There are a limited number of slots available on a first come first served basis. Exhibitor slots may be reserved by contacting Sara Spangelo (spangelo.sara@gmail.com) and we offer two options:
  • $250 Table (3 ft x 8 ft, includes one conference registration)
  • $500 Booth (10 ft x 12 ft+, includes two conference registrations)
    • Power, wireless internet, and black table drapes are provided. Taking into account feedback from last year's conference, we have relocated the exhibitor area to be directly outside the conference auditorium. Coffee breaks will take place in the exhibitor area, ensuring a steady flow of attendee traffic.

    Exhibitor displays and materials may be shipped to Pasadena ahead of time via Caltech Shipping and Receiving, and will be carefully transported to the conference venue by Caltech Transportation Services. We can also work with you to arrange shipping from Caltech to your company after the conference.

Sponsors

    We also invite companies and organizations to sponsor dinner on Monday night, lunches, coffee breaks, or the conference in general. Please contact Sara Spangelo (spangelo.sara@gmail.com) with any questions or to register as a sponsor.
